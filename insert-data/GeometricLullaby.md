*Geometric Lullaby* is a reflection of the soothing sounds that lull us to sleep. Shaped by the nuances extracted from a rich dataset of lullaby lyrics, this work translates the sentiment dynamics of timeless lullabies into form. It's a visual lullaby, which explores the intersection of data and nostalgia.
