Background
So, a little more about me. I love turning things into datasets, the programming language Python, watching kdramas, geometric shapes, and the satisfying feeling after cleaning messy data. I was born and raised in The Hague , Netherlands. I'm a curious and analytical person at heart, and see structure in everything as a habit.
I have always been interested in numbers and patterns, which led me to pursue a Data Science degree in the first place (Master of Science in Data Science, @Tilburg University). There's just something so satisfying about digging into a dataset and finding patterns and connections that others might miss.
Besides puzzling with data and code, I also enjoy creating digital art. Especially glitch art and digital collage art. Did you know that even most of the images used on this website are my own creations?
